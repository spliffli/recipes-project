Tortellini Minestrone Soup Recipe

By alands18

Description

I haven't personally tried this dish yet, but it looks like it could be very addictive during the winter.

It's a soup with mixed vegetables, cheese and tortellini for 8 people.

Ingredients:

  • 680g (1.5 lbs) veggie sausage (Not the original recipe, but this is what I will use)
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups veggie broth
  • 170g (16 Oz.) peeled & chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cups thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • ¾ cup dry red wine
  • 30g package tortellini
  • 2 cups thinly sliced zucchini
  • ¼ cup grated cheese, or to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat a Dutch oven or large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir sausage in the Dutch oven until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is tender, 5 to 10 minutes.

    Step 2

    Stir broth, tomatoes, carrots, celery, ketchup, and red wine into veggie sausage mixture; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until carrots and celery are tender, about 30 minutes. Add tortellini and zucchini to the soup and cover; simmer until zucchini are tender, 25 to 30 more minutes. Ladle soup into serving bowls and top with cheese.
